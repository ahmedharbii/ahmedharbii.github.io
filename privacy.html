Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 9, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to my personal portfolio website. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle information when you visit ahmedharbii.github.io.

Information Collection

This website is a static portfolio site hosted on GitHub Pages. I do not collect, store, or process any personal data through this website.

What We Don't Collect:

  • No email addresses
  • No names or personal information
  • No tracking cookies
  • No analytics data
  • No IP addresses

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies for basic functionality:

  • Theme Preference: Stores your dark/light mode choice (localStorage)
  • Cookie Consent: Remembers your cookie consent choice
  • Service Worker: Enables offline functionality (PWA feature)

These are strictly necessary cookies that enable the website to function properly. No third-party or tracking cookies are used.

Third-Party Services

GitHub Pages

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect visitor information as described in their Privacy Statement.

External Links

This website contains links to external sites (LinkedIn, GitHub, Google Scholar, Twitter). I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

CDN Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts and Three.js from CDN. These services may collect usage data as per their respective privacy policies.

Service Worker & PWA

This website uses a service worker to enable offline functionality. The service worker caches pages and resources locally in your browser for faster loading and offline access. All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Contact Form

When you click on the email link on the contact page, it opens your default email client. No data is submitted through this website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Decline cookies (though this may affect functionality)
  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • Use browser privacy features or ad blockers

Children's Privacy

This website does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

Compliance
